To Our Sonshine Family,

If you've found your way here, it's probably because Sonshine Educational Tours was part of your story — and we're glad you came back.

Maybe you were one of the teachers who trusted Sonshine with your students year after year. Maybe you were a kid who saw the White House for the first time, walked the Freedom Trail in Boston, or stood in front of Independence Hall and felt history come alive. Maybe you were a parent who watched your child come home buzzing with stories they couldn't stop telling.

For over 30 years, Miss Cheryl and the Sonshine team poured their hearts into making those moments happen. Every itinerary was personal. Every phone call mattered. Every trip was built on the belief that the best classroom has no walls — and that every student deserves to see the world beyond their hometown.

Sonshine Educational Tours may have closed its doors, but the experiences it created are still out there — in every student who fell in love with history, every teacher who saw the spark in their kids' eyes, and every memory you carry from those trips.

Cheryl — from all the schools, all the teachers, all the kids, and all the bus rides — thank you. You built something that mattered. And the people whose lives you touched haven't forgotten.

With love and gratitude, from your Sonshine family. ☀
